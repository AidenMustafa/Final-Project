Hollow Knight Statistics

Released on February 24, 2017, Hollow Knight quickly captured the hearts of players worldwide, with over 2,000 players within just a few days. Developed by a small indie team of three (Ari Gibson, William Pellen, and Jack Vine), Team Cherry's dedication brought the game to life, despite the challenges and the 4-year development time. Here are some intriguing statistics about this beloved title:

Total Copies Sold

6.4M

Revenue Generated

$89M

Average Player Playtime

30 Hours

Interesting Facts:

  1. Hollow Knight had a peak of 8,288 players within 24 hours.
  2. On April 30, 2022, the all-time peak reached 20,324 players.
